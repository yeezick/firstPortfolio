Who am I?

I am a 21 year old, self-taught, junior web-developer based in Hollywood, Florida. I aspire to be at least a front-end developer by July 2021. My long-term goal is to become a full-stack Software Engineer, as soon as mentally possible. It's very inspiring to me to think I may end up writing programs for companies like NASA, Tesla, Apple, or maybe even developing high-quality games for a company like EA.

I might have a long way to go but I've already made a dent and I am nowhere near done.
